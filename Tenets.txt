Tenet
(Redirected from Tenets)
Jump to navigationJump to search
Please help with verifying or updating older sections of this article.
At least some were last verified for version 1.18.

This article is for the PC version of Crusader Kings 3 only.

Tenets represent a faith's most important rites, rituals and traditions. Each faith has exactly three.

The piety cost to choose a tenet when creating a new faith depends on what religion it belongs to. The cost of the tenet will also be discounted by 50% if it is present in the original faith.

Syncretism
All faiths can pick a Syncretism tenet, which borrow at least one virtue and sin from another religion, allow the faith to use artifacts restricted to that religion, grants +30  Opinion mutually with all characters and counties following it, and makes their faiths and the faith with the tenet consider each other Astray/Hostile instead of Hostile/Evil, depending on the faiths' Theism doctrine. Each faith can have only one Syncretism tenet.

If a faith has a Syncretism tenet and attempts to convert a county whose Faith matches it, the Task conversion.png County Conversion Speed is reduced by −25%.
