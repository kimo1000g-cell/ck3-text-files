Planning a pilgrimage travel
Activities are special occasions hosted by a certain character. Most activities have multiple variants to choose from. Each activity goes through a number of phases and during each phase there is a chance every 7 days for an event to happen. A character can only take part in one activity as a time and characters with the  Incapable or a disease trait cannot host or join activities.

Most activities requires other characters to join. If not a single character joins the activity will be canceled and the character who started it will gain 20  Stress. After an activity started, characters who were not initially invited can be invited manually via a character interaction.

The character starting an activity is the Host and chooses a barony for the activity, with the cost depending on the barony. The map will pin the best 5 baronies for the activity but any valid barony can be selected. All characters who attend an activity must travel to the designated barony unless it's their realm capital. Each character will travel with an Entourage made up of characters relevant to the activity.

Examinations
DLC All Under Heaven.png	Available only with the All Under Heaven DLC enabled.
Examinations are activities available to adult rulers who have  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government and can only be held in the realm capital. Activity guests can gain the  Confucian Education trait or experience for it but the activity host cannot. Every character who takes an examination has an Exam Score, which determines their chance to pass the examination. The initial Exam Score depends on the experience in the  Confucian Education trait,  Martial,  Stewardship,  Learning, whether they know the host's language, and whether they've been caught cheating before or not. The activity host will have the option to increase the Exam Score of certain characters depending on the activity intent.

Only landless characters can take an examination. If a governor attempts to attend an activity and didn't pass all their examinations it will receive the option to abdicate.

Characters who take an examination can cheat but this has a 90% chance to give a secret and a 10% chance to get caught. If the character is caught it will have the following options to get the secret instead:

Pay 100  Influence.
Attempt an  Intrigue challenge. If the challenge is failed the character will gain +40  Stress.
If a  Friend,  Best Friend or Lover.png Lover is present in the same activity they can be blamed but the relation will change to  Rival.
If none of these options are taken the character will fail the examination, gain -350  Prestige, -60  Merit and a  Stress Level, and for the rest of their life they will have reduced Exam Score and certain event choices during examinations will become unavailable.

If no examination activity has been held in Hegemony of China for the last 6 years, the Dynastic Cycle situation will be pushed towards the Dynastic cycle phase instability.png Tension era each year. The effect is doubled if no examination activity has been held for the last 10 years.

University Visit
DLC Wards & Wardens.png	Available only with the Wards and Wardens DLC enabled.
University Visit is an activity available only to adults who do not have a tier 5 education trait and can only be held every 20 years. It can target any barony that has a university or holy building or is the capital of China. Constantinople also receives a bonus. If the barony's faith considers the character's faith Hostile the activity will cost 200  Piety. If the barony has a Confucian faith, culture with Chinese  Heritage, or is owned by Hegemony of China, completing the activity will give the  Confucian Education trait or experience for it.

The activity has one Activity Option: Books and Notes. Level 2 costs 150  Gold, gives 3 Studiousness and increases a random skill by 1. Level 3 costs 300  Gold, gives with 10 Studiousness, increases a random skill by 1, will increase the amount of  Confucian Education trait experience if the location would give it, and will give a Trinket artifact if the character does not have a Royal Court and a Book artifact if the character has a Royal Court.

University Visits have a meter called Studiousness, which ranges from 0 to 100 and decides which rewards the character will receive when the activity ends. The starting amount depends on the level of Books and Notes. If the barony is Constantinople, it will give an additional 10 points. If the barony has a completed Great project china.png Strengthen Capital great project, it will give an additional 10 points.

Funeral
DLC Legends of the Dead.png	Available only with the Legends of the Dead DLC enabled.
Funeral is an activity available only to rulers who have any dead relative they didn't use the activity on and can only be held every 5 years in any realm barony. It has a single activity option, Ceremonials, which affects the reward. When the activity starts it will give 25  Prestige. When the activity ends it will give the following rewards:

+20  Legitimacy, +200  Piety and -20  Stress if Ceremonials is at level 1
+50  Legitimacy, +250  Piety and -35  Stress if Ceremonials is at level 2
+100  Legitimacy, +350  Piety and -60  Stress if Ceremonials is at level 3
+10  Legitimacy and +50  Piety if the barony has a Holding church.png Temple holding and the same faith
+10  Legitimacy and +50  Piety if the barony has a holy building
+10  Legitimacy and +50  Piety if the barony is a holy site
+10  Legitimacy and +50  Piety if the barony has a Building tower of silence.png Great Tower of Silence building
+10  Legitimacy and +50  Piety if the barony has  Mountains or  Desert Mountains terrain and faith has the Sky Burials doctrine
 Piety rewards are reduced by -90% if faith has the Mummification doctrine and increased by +25% if faith has the Stoic Burial doctrine
 Stress rewards are reduced by -50% if faith has the Stoic Burial doctrine and increased by +200% if faith has the Bewailing Burial doctrine
If the faith has the Mummification doctrine, the host will receive the choice between gaining an artifact or an additional 250  Piety. The rarity of the artifact depends on the traits (especially if they are virtues or sins) and title rank of the deceased as well as whether they were a head of faith. Every ruler can choose the artifact to be a Trinket and rulers who have a Royal Court can choose the artifact to be Furniture instead.

Camp Revelry
Roads to Power	Available only with the Roads to Power DLC enabled.
Camp Revelry is an activity available only to adventurers which lasts 3 months. It has a base cost of 15  Gold and two activity options:

Feast option food.png Food determines the starting Carousing. Increasing it to level 2 or 3 requires a Supply Tent domicile building and can trigger events to pay  Provisions and gain a either  Piety or a follower.

Level 2 requires a Supply Tent domicile building. Level 3 requires the Domicile building brewers.png Mobile Brewers upgrade and gives the Master of Ceremonies modifier for 3 years at the end of the activity.
The ruler of the barony where the camp is located can be invited as a guest of honor. The chance for them to accept decreases with their primary title rank.

Camp Revelries have a meter called Carousing, which ranges from 0 to 100 and decides which rewards the host will receive when the activity ends.

Coronation
DLC Coronations.png	Available only with the Coronations DLC enabled.
Coronation is a highly interactive activity that can only be held once. To have a coronation a ruler must be at least 12 years old, must have a  Kingdom or above title rank, and if the realm has a Ceremonial Monarchy neither monarch can use the activity unless they are the same character. Rulers with  Ritsuryō or  Sōryō government can only use the activity if they are the top liege. Only adult characters can attend. AI rulers will always have a Coronation as soon as they can. The activity has two variants available. Which variant is available to who depends on the faith's Coronation Tradition doctrine.

Coronation type normal icon.png Coronation can be held in either the realm capital or a holy site within borders and is more successful if the barony meets both requirements. If the characters has  Administrative government they must also be independent. It will also give the host the Crowned king.png Crowned Sovereign law unless the Coronation Laws game rule has been set to No Coronation Laws.
Coronation type anointment icon.png Anointment can only be held in the location where the head of faith is present and gives higher rewards. The character's Level of Devotion must be at least Faithful, they must not have the  Excommunicated trait, and the activity variant must be unlocked by requesting it from the head of faith, who must accept. It will also give the host the Crowned emperor.png Anointed Sovereign law unless the Coronation Laws game rule has been set to No Coronation Laws. When the activity ends the faith will gain +4  Fervor.
The activity has two Activity Options. Increasing the level of the Activity Options requires the character to have a Artifact regalia.png Regalia artifact if their religion is Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, Hausa, Finno-Ugric or Slavic and a Artifact crown.png Crown artifact if their religion is any other. At level 2 each one will give +15 Starting Magnificence and at level 3 each one will give +25 Starting Magnificence

Coronation option ceremony feast type.png Ceremony determines how likely guests are to join and at the lowest level only characters inside the realm can join
Coronation option ceremony size.png Celebrations determines the likelihood of certain negative events
If both values are at least at level 2 the dynasty will gain +50  Renown and if both values are at level 3 the dynasty will gain +100  Renown.
Each Coronation activity has a Magnificence value, which ranges from 0 to 100 and is divided into 5 levels. The level reached at the end of the activity determines part of its rewards.

Oath
When a Coronation activity ends the host will also have the option to swear an oath. Swearing an oath will add a decision that gives rewards if completed. The oath is canceled if the character loses its last  Kingdom or higher title. 11 random oaths from the following list can be selected, provided the character meets the requirements.

Failing to fulfill an oath will increase the ruler's  Stress and reduce the their  Legitimacy and  Prestige and  Opinion with every vassal, courtier and guest. It also has a 5% chance to give the ruler the the Oathbreaker nickname.

Both the rewards for fulfilling an oath and the penalties for failing scale with the size of the character's realm and the culture's era.

Blót
DLC Northern Lords.png	Available only with the Northern Lords DLC enabled.
Blót is an activity available only to rulers whose faith has the Gruesome Festivals tenet and can be held every 9 years. It can only be held in counties whose faith also has Gruesome Festivals tenet and how long the activity lasts depends on the size of the realm. The activity costs 200  Prestige in addition to its base cost and has two activity options:

Gruesome festival option size.png Festival Size determines  Opinion bonuses. At levels 2 and 3 every country in the realm that follows the same faith will gain 30 Popular Opinion for 7 years and the host will be able to choose between 50  Opinion with a vassal or increasing the Popular Opinion bonus to 50. At level 3 every vassal who is a  Count or above will gain an additional 20  Opinion.
Gruesome festival option sacrifice.png Sacrifice Fodder determines what will be sacrificed.
Sacrificing animals is free and gives 50  Piety and 30  Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm.
Sacrificing majestic animals costs scaled  Gold and gives 100  Piety and 30  Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm.
Sacrificing humans is free and gives 100  Piety and 30  Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm but characters and counties whose faith does not have the Gruesome Festivals or Human Sacrifice tenets will lose 50  Opinion for 5 years.
Sacrificing majestic humans costs scaled  Gold and gives 250  Piety and 30  Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm but characters and counties whose faith does not have the Gruesome Festivals or Human Sacrifice tenets will lose 50  Opinion.
While the activity is underway and human sacrifice was selected, the Designate Sacrifice interaction will become available with any imprisoned character, which will kill them when the activity ends. Only one prisoner will be sacrificed, chosen at random if the interaction was used on multiple prisoners. If a head of faith is sacrificed, its faith will gain 50  Fervor, holy wars against the activity host will be cheaper, and great holy wars will be more likely to target them. This can only happen once per faith.

If the faith has the Ritual Cannibalism tenet and human sacrifice was selected, every noble character who attends the activity will gain the  Cannibal trait if it doesn't have it already.

If the faith has a  Temporal head of faith, the host will have the option to gain 50  Opinion with them instead of the bonuses from Festival Size.

Chariot Race
Roads to Power	Available only with the Roads to Power DLC enabled.
Chariot Race is a highly interactive activity that can be held every 10 years in the realm capital. It is only available for adult  Emperors and above whose culture has the Roman Ceremonies tradition and is more succesful if the realm capital is Constantinople. The activity has one Activity Option: Spectacles. The option determines how much  Legitimacy and  Popular Opinion will be gained at the end of the activity, which also scales with the existing  Popular Opinion in the realm capital. The activity can include one Honorary Guest, which will make them the target for some events to interact with them.

The activity will also give  Influence to the host if their government uses the resource. If the barony is the realm capital it will gain +20  Development progress.

In the activity will compete 4 teams: Venetoi, Prasinoi, Leukoi and Rhousioi. Each team will be made up of 2 or 3 characters, which will be displayed on the right. Before the race begins the characters will be sorted by the experience of their Charioteer trait.

After all guests arrive the betting will open and every character will have the option to place bets on one of the teams or place no bet. Hovering over the option to place bets will display the team's chances to win and how many guests have bet on that particular team. Once a team is selected the bet can be placed on either one of the team members or the entire team. Then the bet can be made on first place, first two places, or first three places, with the payout increasing if chances are lower. Then the amount of  Gold for the bet must be selected: 50, 100, 150 or 200. If the Charioteer court position.png Champion Charioteer is bet on an wins, it will reward  Influence.

Once the race begins the window on the right will display the place in the race of every charioteer. It will also display which characters were bet on and the Charioteer court position.png Champion Charioteer.

If the Charioteer court position.png Champion Charioteer court position is not already filled there will be an option to spend 60  Influence and appoint one of the charioteers.

At the end of the race every contender will gain experience for the Charioteer trait in the order of their place in the race.

Grand Tournament

A Grand Tournament
Grand Tournament is a highly interactive activity that can be held every 10 years in a Domain barony. It is only available for adult characters. The activity will be paid with  Treasury if the host's government uses the resource and with  Gold otherwise. The activity has two Activity Options:

Tournament option accommodations.png Accommodations determine the likelihood of certain negative events. Can choose to pay 20, 60 or 180  Gold or  Treasury.
Tournament option prizes.png Prizes determines the quality of prize artifacts and whether characters in the top places will receive  Gold. Can choose to pay 20 (Common), 60 (Common-Masterwork), 180 (Masterwork-Famed and Gold rewards), 360 (Masterwork-Illustrious and Gold rewards) or 720 (Famed-Illustrious and Gold rewards)  Gold or  Treasury.
When organizing a tournament one may appoint a Champion. Any rewards the Champion receives will be shared with its liege. If the host is participating in a Melee contest the Champion will always be part of the organizer's team.

3 weeks after the tournament is hosted every participating character will be able to visit various areas to trigger events.

Contests
A Grand Tournament is made up of between 1 and 5 Contests. Each character can choose whether to take part in a contest or merely observe. Taking part in a Contest grants the following rewards:

Every character who qualified will gain the Trait tournament.png Hastiluder trait or experience for it and gain a positive modifier for 5 years.
The character in the 3rd place gains a small amount of  Prestige. If they are an  Accolade and the tournament had 4 or 5 contests their liege will gain a tiny amount of  Prestige as well.
The character in the 2nd place gains a moderate amount of  Prestige. If they are an  Accolade their liege will gain a small amount of  Prestige as well.
The character in the 1st place gains a large amount of  Prestige, an artifact, and a stronger positive modifier for 5 years. If they are an  Accolade their liege will gain a moderate amount of  Prestige as well.
If the host spent at least 180  Gold on Prizes then the contestants in the first 3 places will also receive a scaled amount of  Gold.If they are an  Accolade they will give some or all the  Gold to their liege depending on their  Opinion of the liege.
Characters will have a Progress to Victory bar showing how close they are to winning the current Contest. At least 20 progress is required to quality for a Contest. If the host fails to qualify but a rival does not, the host will have the option to replace the the rival in the contest. This will give -20  Opinion with the rival.
