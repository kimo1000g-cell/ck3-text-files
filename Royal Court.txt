Royal court
Jump to navigationJump to search
This article has been verified for the current PC version (1.19) of the game.


The Seljuk court
The Royal Court represents a ruler's seat of majesty and is available to playable characters of  King or higher rank. If the character has  Administrative,  Celestial,  Meritocratic,  Meritocratic Khanate or  Ritsuryō government they must also be independent. If a character's last title of  Kingdom or higher title is destroyed or taken by a character who already has a Royal Court, the court becomes dormant. If the last title is taken by a character of lower rank, the court will be transferred to the new title owner. The visual appearance of a royal court depends on the architecture aesthetics of the realm capital's culture.

In the Royal Court the character can see its spouse, councilors, and most important courtiers. It can also display Book, Furniture, Throne, Pedestal Item and Wall Ornament artifacts.

By default the royal court appearance depends on the location of the realm capital but can be set manually in the Court Visuals tab if the Royal Court Royal Court DLC is installed.

DLC mechanics
Royal Court	Available only with the Royal Court DLC enabled.
On the court screen, adult rulers who are not traveling or leading an army have the option to Hold Court every 5 years. Holding Court costs 100  Prestige for a  King and 125 for an  Emperor, triggers 3 random petition events and grants 6  Court Grandeur and 50  Legitimacy.

An optional event will take place inside a Royal Court every 24-32 months, indicated by an icon above the involved characters. When such an event takes place, the player will be notified that something is happening within the Royal Court.

Court type
Each ruler will have the option to choose between two court types based on the Culture's ethos. Court types determine some additional bonuses that the court will grant at certain levels of grandeur, as well as which courtier trait a courtier will gain after spending 5 years at a court with grandeur level 5. Changing the court type costs 500  Prestige. Court type also determines which court petition events will be more common. If a ruler's culture or government is changed, they can still keep their old court type.

Tribal court
Tribal and Wanua rulers only have access to the Tribal court type, which grants the following bonuses:

Level 1:  +5% Knight Effectiveness
Level 3: Icon loot.png +20% Army Loot Capacity and  +20% Raid Speed
Level 5:  +25% Dread Gain
Level 7:  +5 Vassal Opinion
Level 10:  −25% Men-at-Arms Maintenance
In addition courtiers and guests will gain +1  Prowess once one year has passed, up to 3 times. Courtiers will gain the courtier trait of Warlike courts.

Nomadic court
Khans of the Steppe	Available only with the Khans of the Steppe DLC enabled.
Nomadic rulers only have access to the Nomadic court type, which grants the following bonuses:

Level 1:  +15% Herd Capacity
Level 2:  +20% Army Loot Capacity and  +20% Raid Speed
Level 3: Trait track hunter.png +25% Hunter Venator Trait Experience Gain
Level 4:  +5% Herd Gain
Level 6:  +10 Natural Dread
Level 8:  +5 Direct Vassal Opinion
Level 10:  +2% Herd Conversion into Horde Riders
In addition courtiers and guests will gain +1  Prowess and +5% Trait track hunter.png Hunter Venator Trait Experience Gain once one year has passed, up to 3 times. Courtiers will gain the courtier trait of Warlike courts.

Court language
Court language is the language spoken by a court. If a ruler knows the same language as their court, the court will gain Court Grandeur based on each county in their realm that speaks the same language. This bonus is increased by +25% if the character's culture also uses the same language.

If multiple royal courts use the same language, they will all gain additional Court Grandeur as well. For the court with the highest current grandeur, the ruler must know the language. For the rest, the ruler knowing the language is not required. This bonus is reduced by -50% if the rulers of the courts follow faiths from different religions and by -25% if they have a different government type.

A court can change its language every 5 years at the cost of 750  Prestige. Said language must be either the ruler's culture language or the court language of another ruler within diplomatic range.

Court amenities
Court Amenities are monthly expenditures that increase a court's grandeur and grant various benefits. The cost of each level of amenities scales with the size and income of a ruler's realm as well as their culture's era and government. The level of Court Amenities can only be raised or lowered once per year. The last 3 levels require Feudal or Clan government. Sumptuary debate
If Icon amenity fashion.png Fashion court amenities are at level 1 or 2 and court type is not Tribal or Nomadic then the ruler unlocks the Accuse of Violating Sumptuary Law character interaction on adult direct vassals, courtiers or guests. It cannot be used on friends or characters who are naked from the Naked Priests special doctrine. The interaction will cause a combined  Diplomacy,  Stewardship and Attraction challenge. The winner has a 90% chance to gain +350  Prestige and -30  Opinion with the loser and a 10% chance to gain a choice between  Weak Hook or inprisonment reason. Afterwards the ruler will gain +2  Intrigue for 2 years but also cannot use the interaction again during that time.

Court grandeur

Court grandeur
Court Grandeur is a measure of how famed a royal court is. Each court will have a baseline Court Grandeur, which is determined by dynasty legacies, court amenities and court artifacts. Court Grandeur will be raised or lowered towards the baseline at a rate of 0.2 per month, which is doubled if the ruler's dynasty unlocked the second Glory legacy track.png Glory dynasty legacy. Court Grandeur can also be raised or lowered by holding court and certain events can affect it. A small amount of Court Grandeur is also lost upon succession. Once enough Court Grandeur has been acquired the grandeur level will increase, granting various benefits. If Court Grandeur falls below a level's required amount the bonuses will be lost in 6 months unless Court Grandeur is raised back. As Court Grandeur increases the court will visually become more decorated.

Newly create courts start with 25 Court Grandeur.
