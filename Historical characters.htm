Historical characters[edit | edit source]

Please help with verifying or updating older sections of this article.
At least some were last verified for version 1.17.

This article is for the PC version of Crusader Kings 3 only.

Historical characters are a new game mechanic introduced in Roads to Power. Representing unlanded historical figures of major importance, these characters will always appears with the dynamic culture and the faith of their place of birth, unless specified otherwise, in a windowed event to the player holding their county of birth. The player is then given the choice between leaving entirely their dynasty to play as the historical character as an adventurer, recruit them into their court with a weak hook, or reject them for a small prestige boon.
