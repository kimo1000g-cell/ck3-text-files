Domiciles are available to characters whose government type goes not require them to own landed titles. The appearance of buildings in a domicile depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the domicile is located. There are five types of domiciles:

 Administrative governments have an Administrative estate
Adventurer.png Adventurer governments have a Camp
 Nomadic governments have a Nomadic capital
 Ritsuryō and  Sōryō governments have a Manor
 Celestial,  Meritocratic and  Meritocratic Khanate governments have a Celestial estate

Overview
DLC Roads to Power.png Camp
DLC Roads to Power.png Administrative estate
DLC All Under Heaven.png Celestial estate
DLC All Under Heaven.png Manor
DLC Khans of the Steppe.png Nomadic capital
Every head of a Noble Family with  Administrative government gains an Estate. While at peace the Estate can be moved every 6 months at a cost of  Gold that scales with the Villa's level. The appearance of buildings in the Estate window depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the Estate is located.

The Estate can have various buildings constructed. All buildings take 2 years to build or upgrade

Level 1 buildings cost either 120 or 180  Gold
Level 2 buildings cost either 240 or 360  Gold
Level 3 buildings cost either 360 or 540  Gold
Level 4 buildings cost either 480 or 720  Gold
Level 5 buildings cost either 600 or 900  Gold
Level 6 buildings cost either 720 or 1080  Gold
All Estates will start partially upgraded depending on the start date. In realms that switch government later all Estates will start with a level 1-3 Villa, depending on whether the culture has the innovation to upgrade it.

If the start date was 867 all Estates in realms that start with  Administrative government start with a Domicile building villa.png Villa.
If the start date was 1066 all Estates in realms that start with  Administrative government start with a Domicile building manor.png Manor and three level 1 buildings.
If the start date was 1178 all Estates in realms that start with  Administrative government start with a Domicile building mansion.png Mansion and four level 2 buildings.

Reception Hall
The Reception Hall upgrade is unlocked by either the third Administrative legacy track.png Bureaucracy legacy or by being part of the Conservative political movement in the Dynastic Cycle. Whenever a House members hosts a  Feast or Activity wedding.png Grand Wedding in the barony where the estate is located it adds +10  Legitimacy to the character who inherits the primary title. The counter is reset upon succession.

Cabinet of Curiosities
The Cabinet of Curiosities upgrade is unlocked by the fourth Administrative legacy track.png Bureaucracy legacy. It gives powerful bonuses but each upgrade requires a certain Quality, which is increased by putting artifacts inside from the upgrade menu. Doing this permanently removes the artifacts from the ruler. The amount of Quality increased is equal to each artifact's Rarity. The upgrade also allows the Request as House Head interaction to choose Artifacts and increases the quality of sponsored Inspirations.

A fully upgraded camp
Roads to Power
Available only with the Roads to Power DLC enabled.
All adventurers have a Camp. The number of upgrades that can be added is equal to the level of the building. Many upgrades require a certain camp purpose. The appearance of buildings in the Camp window depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the Camp is located.

The camp can have various buildings constructed. All buildings can be given various upgrades and have 6 levels.

Level 1 buildings cost 60  Gold and take 60 days to build
Level 2 buildings cost 120  Gold and take 70 days to build
Level 3 buildings cost 180  Gold and take 80 days to build
Level 4 buildings cost 240  Gold and take 90 days to build
Level 5 buildings cost 300  Gold and take 100 days to build
Level 6 buildings cost 360  Gold and take 120 days to build
If a building or upgrade is demolished half of its construction cost is refunded. This applies if the adventurer becomes landed as well.

A nomadic capital
Khans of the Steppe
Available only with the Khans of the Steppe DLC enabled.
Every character with  Nomadic government has a Nomadic Capital. All buildings can be given various upgrades. If the character changes government half of the construction cost of every building and upgrade is refunded. The Nomadic Capital can have various buildings and upgrades constructed.

A Nomadic Capital has its own faith and culture, separate from both its owner and its location. A character with a different faith or culture from its Nomadic Capital will unlock decisions to convert either themselves or their Nomadic Capital.

All Nomadic Capitals will start partially upgraded depending on the start date and title rank.

 Counts start with a Ramchackle Main Yurt
 Dukes start with a Small Main Yurt and one level 2 building
 Kings start with a Small Main Yurt and one level 2 building in 867 and 1066 and a Sturdy Main Yurt and two level 2 buildings in 1178
 Emperors start with a Sturdy Main Yurt and two level 2 buildings in 867 and 1066 and a Great Main Yurt and three level 3 buildings in 1178

Building construction
All buildings can be given various upgrades. Both buildings and upgrades have 6 levels.

Level 1 buildings cost 20  Gold, 60  Prestige, 300  Herd, and take 3 months to build. They have 1 upgrade slot.
Level 2 buildings cost 44  Gold, 102  Prestige, 400  Herd, and take 6 months to build. They have 1 upgrade slot.
Level 3 buildings cost 78  Gold, 141  Prestige, 500  Herd, and take 9 months to build. They have 2 upgrade slots.
Level 4 buildings cost 128  Gold, 178  Prestige, 600  Herd, and take 10 months to build. They have 3 upgrade slots and give +1 Defender Advantage to all domain counties.
Level 5 buildings cost 208  Gold, 214  Prestige, 700  Herd, and take 12 months to build. They have 4 upgrade slots and give +1 Defender Advantage to all domain counties.
Level 6 buildings cost 335  Gold, 251  Prestige, 800  Herd, and take 14 months to build. They have 5 upgrade slots and give +2 Defender Advantage to all domain counties.
Level 1 upgrades cost 12  Gold, 32  Prestige, 150  Herd, and take 1 month to build.
Level 2 upgrades cost 20  Gold, 54  Prestige, 200  Herd, and take 2 months to build.
Level 3 upgrades cost 32  Gold, 75  Prestige, 250  Herd, and take 3 months to build.
Level 4 upgrades cost 52  Gold, 95  Prestige, 300  Herd, and take 4 months to build.
Level 5 upgrades cost 84  Gold, 114  Prestige, 350  Herd, and take 5 months to build.
Level 6 upgrades cost 134  Gold, 134  Prestige, 400  Herd, and take 11 months to build.

DLC Khans of the Steppe.png Nomadic capital
Every head of a Noble Family with  Ritsuryō or  Sōryō government gains a Manor. While at peace the Manor can be moved every 6 months at a cost of  Gold that scales with the Mansion's level. The appearance of buildings in the Manor window depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the Manor is located. Constructing or upgrading anything in the Manor takes 2 years.

All Manors will start partially upgraded depending on the start date. In realms that switch government later all Manorswill start with a level 1-3 Mansion, depending on whether the culture has the innovation to upgrade it.

If the start date was 867 all Manors in realms that start with  Ritsuryō or  Sōryō government start with a Domicile building manor 01.png Mansion.
If the start date was 1066 all Manors in realms that start with  Ritsuryō or  Sōryō government start with a Domicile building manor 02.png Large Mansion and three level 1 buildings.
If the start date was 1178 all Manors in realms that start with  Ritsuryō or  Sōryō government start with a Domicile building manor 03.png Manor and four level 2 buildings.

Building construction
All buildings can be given various upgrades. Both buildings and upgrades have 6 levels.

Level 1 buildings cost 45  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 54  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 3 months to build and have 1 upgrade slot.
Level 2 buildings cost 67  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 81  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 6 months to build and have 1 upgrade slot.
Level 3 buildings cost 101  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 122  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 9 months to build and have 2 upgrade slots.
Level 4 buildings cost 135  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 162  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 12 months to build and have 2 upgrade slots.
Level 5 buildings cost 191  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 230  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 15 months to build and have 3 upgrade slots.
Level 6 buildings cost 270  Gold if the Manor is not in the top liege's capital and 324  Gold if the Manor is in the top liege's capital. They take 18 months to build and have 3 upgrade slots.
Level 1 upgrades cost 30  Gold and take 1 month to build.
Level 2 upgrades cost 60  Gold and take 2 months to build.
Level 3 upgrades cost 90  Gold and take 3 months to build.
Level 4 upgrades cost 120  Gold and take 4 months to build.
Level 5 upgrades cost 150  Gold and take 6 months to build.
Level 6 upgrades cost 180  Gold and take 9 months to build.

Every head of a Noble Family with  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government gains an Estate. While at peace the Estate can be moved every 6 months at a cost of  Gold that scales with the Villa's level. The appearance of buildings in the Estate window depends on the character's culture and the terrain depends on the terrain of the barony where the Estate is located.

The Estate can have various buildings constructed.

Most level 1 buildings cost 60  Gold although a few cost 90  Gold. All of them take 12 months to build and have 1 upgrade slot.
Most level 2 buildings cost 120  Gold although a few cost 180  Gold. All of them take 14 months to build and have 2 upgrade slot.
Most level 3 buildings cost 180  Gold although a few cost 270  Gold. All of them take 16 months to build and have 2 upgrade slots.
Most level 4 buildings cost 240  Gold although a few cost 360  Gold. All of them take 18 months to build and have 3 upgrade slots.
Most level 5 buildings cost 300  Gold although a few cost 450  Gold. All of them take 20 months to build and have 3 upgrade slots.
Most level 6 buildings cost 360  Gold although a few cost 540  Gold. All of them take 22 months to build and have 4 upgrade slots.
Level 1 upgrades cost 30  Gold and take 6 months to build.
Level 2 upgrades cost 60  Gold and take 8 months to build.
Level 3 upgrades cost 90  Gold and take 10 months to build.
Level 4 upgrades cost 120  Gold and take 12 months to build.
All new realms will start with a partially upgraded Estate depending on the start date. In realms that switch government later all Estates will start with a level 1-3 Villa, depending on whether the culture has the innovation to upgrade it.

If the start date was 867 all Estates in realms that start with  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government start with a Domicile building manor 01.png Villa.
If the start date was 1066 all Estates in realms that start with  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government start with a Domicile building manor 02.png Manor and three level 1 buildings.
If the start date was 1178 all Estates in realms that start with  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government start with a Domicile building manor 03.png Mansion and four level 2 buildings.

Cabinet of Curiosities
The Cabinet of Curiosities upgrade is unlocked by the fourth Administrative legacy track.png Bureaucracy legacy. It gives powerful bonuses but each upgrade requires a certain Quality, which is increased by putting artifacts inside from the upgrade menu. Doing this permanently removes the artifacts from the ruler. The amount of Quality increased is equal to each artifact's Rarity. The upgrade also allows the Request as House Head interaction to choose Artifacts and increases the quality of sponsored Inspirations.
