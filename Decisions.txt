Page information
 
 Decisions are actions that can be taken under certain circumstances. Some decisions can only be taken once per character or once per game while others can be taken repeatedly. Most decisions are unavailable if the character has the  Incapable trait. Decisions are not shown unless the character meets one of the more specific requirements.

Decisions are divided into three groups:

Character decisions are available to every character in the game
Ruler decisions are available only to rulers - characters who own landed titles
Adventurer decisions are available only to adventurers

Petition the Liege
Royal Court	Available only with the Royal Court DLC enabled.
Adult vassals of a character with a royal court will unlock the Petition the Liege decision every 5 years. Characters who are on an activity and ceremonial monarchs cannot use this decision. If the vassal has  Celestial,  Meritocratic or  Meritocratic Khanate government then their Merit Rank must also be at least 7.

The decision costs between 50 and 250  Prestige or  Influence based on the title rank and government type of the vassal taking the decision. It will also give 20  Stress if the character has the  Arrogant or  Shy trait.

The chance for the liege to accept depends on its  Opinion of the vassal, relations with the vassal, the vassal's  Influence and Merit Rank, and whether the vassal is a powerful vassal or part of a powerful family. The Hegemon of China is also less likely to accept petitions. The liege may also demand  Gold, a  Weak Hook, renouncing any hook the vassal has on them, or leaving any faction the vassal is part of. If the liege accepts both dynasties will gain +50  Renown but if the liege refuses their dynasty will lose -50  Renown.

Escape from Prison
If a character is in prison, almost all decisions are disabled but the Escape from Prison decision is enabled. The success chance depends on the character's  Prowess against the jailor's  Intrigue. The success chance is decreased if the character has the  Craven,  Weak,  Delicate,  Frail or  Feeble or if the character is in the dungeon. The decision will also give -30  Opinion with the jailor for 10 years.

If the escape attempt fails the character will either get a moderate  Health penalty for 5 years or get wounded.

If a  Friend,  Best Friend or close family member over the age of 10 is imprisoned by the same jailor, taking the decision has a chance to make them escape regardless of whether the character's escape attempt is succesful or not.
